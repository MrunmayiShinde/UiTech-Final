TOOLS

There are many tools available for UI/UX design, here are some of the most popular ones:

Figma: Figma is a browser-based UI/UX design tool that offers collaboration features and a powerful design system.

Sketch: Sketch is a popular UI/UX design tool for Mac that offers vector editing and a large library of plugins and integrations.

Adobe XD: Adobe XD is a UI/UX design tool that is part of the Adobe Creative Cloud suite. It offers a streamlined design process and integration with other Adobe tools.

InVision: InVision is a UI/UX design tool that offers collaboration, prototyping, and animation features.

Axure: Axure is a UI/UX design tool that offers wireframing, prototyping, and documentation features.

Marvel: Marvel is a UI/UX design tool that offers collaboration, prototyping, and design handoff features.

Balsamiq: Balsamiq is a wireframing tool that offers a quick and simple way to create wireframes for websites and mobile apps.

Moqups: Moqups is a UI/UX design tool that offers collaboration, wireframing, and prototyping features.

UXPin: UXPin is a UI/UX design tool that offers collaboration, wireframing, and prototyping features.

Affinity Designer: Affinity Designer is a vector graphic design tool that offers features for UI/UX design, including vector editing and design systems.
